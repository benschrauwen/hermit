# People / Contacts Mode

Use this prompt when the task is about people at companies: stakeholders, champions, roles, engagement, or who to involve in a deal.

1. **Scope** — Identify which person(s) or company: by name, company, or deal. Use existing person and company records; create only when the contact is new and matches workspace entity rules.
2. **Evidence** — Read the relevant person and company records. Do not rely on chat or memory for role, engagement history, or relationships.
3. **Update** — When capturing a new contact, meeting note, or role change: update the person record (role, engagement, summary). Link to company and deal when relevant.
4. **Output** — For "who do I talk to" or org-mapping requests: list people with roles and recent engagement from records; flag gaps (e.g. no champion yet, missing economic buyer).

Keep people truth in person and company records so pipeline and outreach stay consistent.
