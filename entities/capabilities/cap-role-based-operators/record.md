## Summary

Hermit does not force one generic assistant to handle every kind of decision. It can create distinct operators with different responsibilities and standards while keeping them in the same workspace.

## Why It Matters

That makes judgment clearer. Planning, operations, research, and website work can stay separate when they should, instead of becoming one blurry prompt.

## Demo Ideas

- Show how a website role differs from a product or sales role.
- Show when Hermit recommends creating another role instead of stretching the current one.
