## What Hermit actually builds

Hermit starts as a local repo and becomes a working application through conversation.

Instead of handing you a generic assistant with a hidden database, it creates a job-specific system inside the workspace itself: roles, markdown records, prompts, workflows, and a read-only UI all live together in one place.

That means the app is inspectable from day one. The files are not an export of the system. They are the system.
