## Why show the internal state at all?

Because Hermit's thesis is that the working system should stay visible.

A normal product website hides the planning process behind a CMS, project board, and internal notes. Hermit can collapse those layers into the same repo. The public pages, internal records, and operator workflow can all be inspected together.

That gives evaluators two things at once:

- a cleaner outside-facing explanation of the product
- a direct look at how the product improves itself over time
