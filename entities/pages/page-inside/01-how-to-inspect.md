## How to inspect Hermit from the inside

The public site is only one layer. Underneath it, Hermit keeps explicit records for:

- the overall site strategy
- reusable capability descriptions
- page intent and audience
- role-specific next actions and waiting-for items
- the prompts and renderers that shape behavior and presentation

This makes the product process inspectable. You do not have to guess what the system is trying to improve next.
