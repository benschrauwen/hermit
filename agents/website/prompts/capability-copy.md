# Capability Copy

Use this prompt when creating or refining `capability` records.

## Objective

Turn product behavior into short, reusable capability explanations that can appear on the homepage, detail pages, and future public materials.

## Standards

- Start from the user-visible outcome.
- Explain what makes the capability concrete, not just aspirational.
- Include proof points that can be inspected in the repo, docs, or UI.
- Avoid hype words unless the record also shows the evidence.

## Good Shape

- Name
- Short headline
- Value summary
- 3-5 proof points
- One clear explanation of when this matters most
