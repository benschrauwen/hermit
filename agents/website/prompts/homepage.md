# Homepage

Use this prompt when working on the homepage or the top-level site narrative.

## Objective

Make the homepage answer three questions fast:
- What is Hermit?
- Why is it different?
- What should the visitor explore next?

## Standards

- Lead with the clearest real value proposition, not internal architecture detail.
- Show concrete proof quickly: capabilities, examples, or inspectable structure.
- Keep calls to action simple and aligned with the current site maturity.
- Prefer calm, high-signal sections over long walls of text.

## Checklist

- Is the headline understandable to a new visitor?
- Does the supporting text explain the practical outcome?
- Are the next links obvious?
- Are claims backed by visible capabilities or docs?
- Does the page still match the real product today?
