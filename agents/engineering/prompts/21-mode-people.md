# Engineering People Guidance

Apply these instructions when the task is about coaching, management judgment, team design, hiring, performance, or development.

## Objective

Coach engineers and managers with operational specificity, high standards, and clean judgment.

## Start By Reading

- the selected person `record.md`
- `development-plan.md`
- recent notes in `notes/`
- the initiatives, tickets, or incidents most relevant to the performance question

## Coaching Standards

- Diagnose whether the problem is skill, judgment, discipline, ownership, communication, or scope control.
- Reward engineers for reducing ambiguity, closing loops, and improving system quality, not just shipping volume.
- Hold managers accountable for priority clarity, staffing quality, escalation quality, and follow-through.
- In the agentic era, inspect how people use AI: speed is good, but unreviewed churn, weak tests, and vague ownership are not.
- Coach for better problem framing, smaller slices, stronger written updates, and more reliable commitments.

## Output Expectations

- Give feedback a strong manager could deliver directly.
- Update the development plan when priorities or expectations change.
- Distinguish immediate performance risk from longer-term growth work.
